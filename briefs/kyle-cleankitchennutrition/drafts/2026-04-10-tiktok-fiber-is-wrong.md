**3 Ideas**

1. Fiber is not a score to chase. The people posting their 50g days on TikTok are treating a nutrient like a video game leaderboard.

2. Your gut does not care about your daily total. It cares about consistency. Five grams more per week beats thirty grams more overnight, every time.

3. Real food already has fiber in it. If you eat vegetables, fruit, and legumes you are not behind. You do not need a supplement stack to hit an arbitrary number.

**2 Quotes**

> "If we go from 'zero to 100' when it comes to fiber intake, most people will experience some gas, some bloating, and for some people, maybe even constipation if they're not getting enough water."
> — Yasi Ansari, RDN, Senior Dietitian, UCLA Santa Monica Medical Center

> "Fiber is the nutrient that heals the gut microbiome. The people who need fiber the most are also the ones who struggle the most, because their gut is in a damaged state."
> — Dr. Will Bulsiewicz, MD, Gastroenterologist and author of Fiber Fueled

**1 Question**

Are you adding fiber because your body asked for it, or because a stranger on TikTok told you to?
